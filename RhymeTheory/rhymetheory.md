where `tail(word)` = substring from last primary-stressed vowel (V1) to the end.

## üéØ Slant Rhymes (a.k.a. Near Rhyme / Imperfect Rhyme)

Slant rhymes share **similar but not identical** sounds. They create softer rhyme effects common in hip-hop, songwriting, modern poetry.

### Types of Slant Rhymes

| Type | Rule | Example | Why it works |
|------|------|---------|--------------|
| **Vowel Rhyme (Assonance)** | Vowel matches, consonant differs | *seen* / *feet* | /IY1 N/ vs /IY1 T/ |
| **Consonant Rhyme** | Ending consonants match | *milk* / *walk* | final /K/ |
| **Pararhyme** | Consonants match, vowel differs | *pad* / *pod* | P-D same |
| **Weak Rhyme** | Stress mismatch | *writer* / *fighter* | unstressed vs stressed |
| **Family Rhyme** | Similar mouth placement | *dad* / *bad* | both alveolar stops |
| **Wrenched Rhyme** | Forced pronunciation | *home* / *come* | vowel tweak |
